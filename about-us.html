About Global Associates

Founded In Andhra Pradesh, Global Associates (GA) is an ISO-certified startup committed to revolutionizing Security Management and services across India. Our mission is to protect INDIA.

Global Associates is a direct supplier of security cameras, video surveillance systems, and CCTV equipment. We supply analog CCTV cameras, HD security cameras, Network (IP) cameras, and complete video surveillance systems worldwide.

We supply our equipment to homeowners, business owners, government agencies, and any other type of organization any size. Most of our business comes from the Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, however, we do ship our products everywhere in the India. We can handle small projects to too large projects. We have trained engineers that can help design a system that will fit your requirements and budget.

Global Associates has many pre-configured security camera systems available on our store. For example, you can find our Best Selling 4K Network camera systems with AI software here. You can find our 1080p BNC / CCTV camera systems here. However, many customers have specific requirements that may not fit into one of our pre-packaged systems. Maybe you already have existing cable in place that you want to use. Or maybe you need to mix and match a variety of different style cameras, or some 2mp cameras and some 4k cameras.
